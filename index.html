Welcome to the Weather Data Summary

Author: Maria Quinton

Summary Tables of Latitude and Weather - Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness and Wind Speed

This project analyzed how weather is affected based on its latitude or as you get closer or further from the equator.The project used data from the OpenWeather API to create a dataset for over 600 cities.

After compiling and finding the necessary data, the information was plotted using Matplotlib and pandas. The plots and data examined: temperature, humidity, cloudiness and wind speed.
