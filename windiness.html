Windiness Summary vs. Latitude

This page displays the summary analysis of the relationship between wind speed and latitude. From the plot you can see there appears to be a random correlation between wind speed and latitude. The wind speeds are fairly distributed across the latitudes, but the highest speeds appear to be at the 45th parallel, which cuts through the Northern part of the United States.
